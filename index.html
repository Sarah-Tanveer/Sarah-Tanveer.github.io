About Me!

Hey! I’m Sarah. I’m a computer scientist, researcher, student and an avid crocheter! I am on the market for MS with Thesis in Computer Science, with a focus on Networks and Systems. I am motivated by problems that enable underserved communities to access the Web and prompte Tech Equity but I am still exploring different research topics in networks. Currently, I am a Research Intern at the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign with Prof. Deepak Vasisht and Prof. Indranil Gupta. My work here revolves around short-form video content and LEO Satellites. I completed my Bachelor’s in Computer Science from LUMS where I was advised by Prof. Zafar Ayyub Qazi, Prof. Ihsan Ayyub Qazi and Prof. Zartash Afzal Uzmi. My work with them revolved around Web Equity and Affordability. I spent a summer as a Software Engineering Intern for Tajir (YC W20).

Other than that, one of my passions is to uplift women in STEM, especially in developing countries. I was the General Secretary of LUMS Women in Computing (2022-2023) where I conducted several events to empower women in tech. I have been affiliated with them for the past three years and will continue to support our cause! Check out our Instagram page for the cool stuff that we do: LWiC

Research Experience

  • Web Affordability and Inclusion (Jan 2022 - May 2023)
    - Conducted a large-scale measurement study of 72,000 webpages to analyze the affordability of the Web
    - Proposed and implemented a novel framework (transcoding service) to decrease average webpage sizes with minimal impact to quality
    - Designed and implemented a novel heuristics-based image reduction algorithm and a brute force algo- rithm to reduce webpage size with minimal quality degradation
    - Systematically analyzed the effectiveness of the framework showing that it could be used to improve affordability of webpages
    - First Author Publication at SIGCOMM 2023

  • Short-Form Video Caching (Sept 2023 - Dec 2023)
    - Under the guidance of Prof. Deepak Vasisht and Prof. Indranil Gupt at UIUC
    - Studying system design to serve short-form video content
    - Implemented a system to test out various caching schemes for short-form video content

  • LEO Satellites (Sept 2023 - Dec 2023)
    - Under the guidance of Prof. Deepak Vasisht and Prof. Indranil Gupt at UIUC
    - Exploring network properties of Starlink
    - Collected measurements to analyze the throughput pattern of Starlink and how it corresponds to the relative position of the satellite
